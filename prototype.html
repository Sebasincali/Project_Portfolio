Key Design Features

Peer Connection

Intuitive interface for students to connect with tutors and join study groups

Smart Search

Advanced filtering by course, subject, and availability for quick tutor discovery

Scheduling System

Easy-to-use calendar interface for booking sessions at campus locations

Discussion Forum

Organized by course levels with photo attachments and threaded discussions

Verified Tutors

Display tutor credentials, courses taken, and student ratings for trust

Notifications

Real-time alerts for session reminders, new responses, and tutor availability
